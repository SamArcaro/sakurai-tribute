Masahiro Sakurai (桜井 政博)

Game Director | Game Designer | Scenario Writer

Here's a time line of the games that Masahiro Sakurai has worked on:

  • 1992 - Kirby's Dream Land
  • 1993 - Kirby's Adventure
  • 1996 - Kirby Super Star
  • 1999 - Super Smash Bros.
  • 2000 - Kirby 64: The Crystal Shards
  • 2001 - Super Smash Bros. Melee
  • 2002 - Kirby: Nightmare in Dream Land
  • 2003 - Kirby Air Ride
  • 2004 " - Kirby & the Amazing Mirror"
  • 2005 - Meteos
  • 2006 - Sodatete! Kouchuu Ouja Mushiking
  • 2008 - Super Smash Bros. Brawl
  • 2012 - Kid Icarus: Uprising
  • 2014 - Super Smash Bros. for Nintendo 3DS and Wii U
  • 2018 - Super Smash Bros. Ultimate

"A game's concept is one of its most important assets. I feel that we must aim to satisfy both beginners and experts, and that hasn't changed since I was making 'Kirby' games."

-- Masahiro Sakurai
